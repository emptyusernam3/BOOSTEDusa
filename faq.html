FAQ Section (Frequently Asked Questions) - Boosted USA
Question: What models of electronic scooters and skateboards does Boosted USA offer?
Answer: Boosted USA offers a wide range of models of electronic scooters and skateboards. Question: What are the advantages of Boosted USA e-scooters and skateboards?
Answer: Boosted USA's e-scooters and skateboards offer a unique combination of style, performance and innovation. They are equipped with powerful electric motors that ensure smooth and fast movement. In addition, our models have high maneuverability, long battery life and the ability to adjust the speed. They are ideal for both everyday trips and outdoor adventures.
Question: What are the battery capabilities of Boosted USA e-scooters and skateboards?
Answer: The batteries used in our models have a high capacity and provide a long working time. Depending on the specific model and conditions of use, you can expect to run from 10 to 20 miles (16-32 km) on a single battery charge. The charging time is approximately 1 to 2 hours, depending on the model and the charger used.
Question: What are the speed control options for Boosted USA e-scooters and skateboards?
Answer: All models of Boosted USA electronic scooters and skateboards offer speed control. You can choose a comfortable speed level that suits your preferences and road conditions. Usually, our models have several speed modes that allow you to control the speed of movement.

Quick Start Guide

  • Unboxing and Assembly:

    Carefully open the package and remove all the components.

    Follow the provided instructions to assemble your Boosted Rev.

    Make sure all parts are securely attached before proceeding.

  • Powering On:

    Locate the power button on the scooter.

    Press and hold the power button until the display turns on.

    The scooter will perform a brief self-check and be ready for use.

  • Acceleration and Braking:

    To accelerate, gently twist the throttle located on the handlebars.

    Gradually increase the throttle for a smooth and controlled ride.

    To brake, apply pressure to the brake lever located on the handlebars.

  • Riding Modes:

    Boosted Rev offers different riding modes to suit your preference.

    Use the mode button to switch between modes (e.g., Beginner, Eco, Sport).

    Start with a lower mode if you're new to electric scooters and gradually increase as you become more comfortable.

  • Safety and Precautions:

    Always wear a helmet and other protective gear while riding.

    Observe traffic rules and ride in designated areas.

    Be cautious of pedestrians and other vehicles on the road.

    Regularly check the scooter's tires, brakes, and battery level for optimal performance.
